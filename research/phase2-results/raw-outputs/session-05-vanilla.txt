## Cost Optimization Strategy

**Selected Approach: Warm Standby**
- Maintains 1 warm task per region (2 total)
- Cost: $22/month for both standbys
- Eliminates cold start delays
- Scales down to 1 (not 0) to maintain readiness

**Alternatives Considered:**
- Always-on (10 idle tasks): $110/month
- Scale-to-zero: $0 idle cost but 30-second cold start delay
- **Savings: $88/month vs. always-on approach**
