### Thread 5: Steering Prompt Builder
- **Function**: `buildSteeringPrompt()` in `/home/node/.openclaw/workspace/repos/kasett-rewind/src/index.ts`
- **Input**: ThreadAnalysis (core/fresh/fading) + previous metas
- **Output format**: Narrative summary + `[THREAD_META]` block
- **Block structure**:
  ```
  [THREAD_META]
  main: <description>
  sub1: <description>
  sub2: <description>
  sub3: <description>
  [/THREAD_META]
  ```
- **Fallback**: With no previous metas, produces basic format instructions only

### Thread 6: THREAD_META Parser
- **Function**: `parseCompactionOutput()`
- **Regex**: `/\[THREAD_META\]\s*\n([\s\S]*?)\n?\s*\[\/THREAD_META\]/`
- **Parses**: `main:`, `sub1:`, `sub2:`, `sub3:` lines
- **Returns**: `{summary: string, meta: ThreadMeta|null}`
- **Fallback**: If no block found, returns `{summary: raw_output.trim(), meta: null}`

## Phase 1 Benchmark Results
- **Conditions**: Vanilla vs. Kasett-steered summarization
- **Sessions**: 10 synthetic sessions across 3 tiers
- **Results**:

- **Conclusion**: Kasett steering significantly improves both thread retention and key state survival
