# Conversation Summary

## Project Overview
The team is conducting a replication study of the LoCoMo dataset to compare against their own approach, with plans to submit results to EMNLP by the June 15 deadline (approximately 5 weeks away).

## Key Technical Details

### LoCoMo Dataset
- **Source**: https://huggingface.co/datasets/snap-stanford/locomo
- **Size**: ~1.2GB compressed
- **Sessions**: 300 sessions in eval split
- **Baseline**: 67.3% accuracy on temporal QA subset (target to beat/match)
- **Authors**: First author Maharana, with coauthors Lee, Tulyakov, and Bansal (2024)

### Implementation Specifications
- **Model**: Sonnet 4.5 (pinned across all conditions for clean comparison with phase 2 data)
- **BERTScore Version**: 0.3.13 (pinned; v0.4 breaks rescale baseline comparability)
- **Entry Point**: `node research/locomo-replication/run-eval.mjs --pilot`
- **Results Directory**: `research/locomo-replication/results/` (same structure as phase2-results layout)
- **Evaluation**: ~3 API calls per session

### Execution Constraints
- **Rate Limits**: OpenRouter caps at 50 RPM on Sonnet
- **Timeline**: 300 sessions = 2-3 days of throughput due to rate limits
- **Initial Run**: 50-session pilot subset planned first

## Key Comparisons
The main methodological difference identified: LoCoMo evaluates on cleaner data, while the team's tier-3 sessions are more realistic. This distinction should be mentioned in the related work section.
