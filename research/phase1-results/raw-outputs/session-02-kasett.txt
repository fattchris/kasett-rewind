## Conversation Summary

A production auth incident was fully resolved. Here's what happened:

**Incident:** After deploying v3.1.0, 340 users received 401 errors over ~70 minutes when refreshing tokens at `https://auth.moltaicorp.com/v1/tokens/refresh`.

**Root Cause:** A regression in `/opt/services/auth-service/src/middleware/jwt.ts` changed the JWT expiry check to compare `Date.now()` (milliseconds) directly against `token.exp` (seconds), making all tokens appear immediately expired.

**Resolution:**
- Hotfix built: changed comparison to `Date.now() / 1000 > token.exp`
- Tagged and deployed as `v3.1.0-hotfix`
- Verified via session `a3f8c201-9d44-4b7e-8c22-1f0e3b5d9a77` returning HTTP 200
- Endpoint confirmed stable, error rate back to baseline
- Affected users can re-authenticate naturally; no manual backfill needed

**Prevention Measures Added:**
- New unit test: `'should reject tokens with expiry in milliseconds'` in `jwt.test.ts`
- ESLint rule added to catch direct `Date.now()` vs JWT `exp` comparisons statically

**Documentation Completed:**
- Incident log updated
- Postmortem written and sent to team
- Incident archived
